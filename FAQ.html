The Story So Far

In a fractured world haunted by mysterious robotic beasts known as Unidentifiables, salvation comes in the form of ancient, towering Mechas—machines that can only be summoned through a rare bond with celestial beings known as Angels. Micah, a young man with a troubled past, is chosen by an elusive Angel named Koh, who detests humanity and refuses to trust him. As Micah struggles to survive in a new city that trains him in basic combat, he must also confront the cold indifference of his own Angel in hopes of forging a bond strong enough to awaken their shared Mecha. Alongside a diverse cast—Bailey, a noble fighter with a rapier; Indigo, a desert-born marksman; Kentaro, a disciplined swordsman; and Ardi, a brilliant young scientist—the group learns that their individual battles are part of a much larger war. With spirits that can fight beside them and Mechas reserved for the gravest threats, the team must learn to balance power, trust, and unity before the next wave of destruction falls.

FAQ

Q: What is Angels of Earth?
A: Angels of Earth is a futuristic, character-driven story set in a world where colossal robotic beings known as Unidentifiables threaten humanity’s survival. To combat them, a select few individuals—each chosen by enigmatic, sentient spirits called Angels—are granted access to ancient, divine Mechas. These Mechas can only be summoned when there is deep trust and union between human and Angel. The story follows Micah, a young man with a complex past, who is bonded with Koh, an aloof and reluctant Angel who distrusts humanity. Alongside a diverse main cast—each wielding distinct weapons, backstories, and emotional scars—Micah journeys through war-torn lands, class divisions, and spiritual revelations. As they fight to uncover the truth behind the Unidentifiables and the origins of the Angels themselves, they’re also forced to confront their inner demons and question what it truly means to protect the Earth—not just from destruction, but from losing its soul.
